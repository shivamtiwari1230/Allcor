About Allcor

Allcor has established an exceptional reputation for its innovative design, superior quality, and diverse color options. We are committed to providing cutting-edge colors and designs that distinguish our PVC laminates from the competition. Our unique palette and patterns enhance any application, delivering a notable visual impact.

Our latest collection includes an array of solid colors, wood grains, and directional patterns inspired by the elegance of marble and granite, replicating the sophisticated appearance of natural stone. As a leading supplier across India, Allcor not only offers the most contemporary designs and colors but also adheres to the highest manufacturing standards, thereby elevating thequality of life for our customers.

Journey

At Allcor, our journey began with a vision to provide high-quality PVC laminates that seamlessly blend style and durability. In our early days, we focused on establishing a solid foundation with essential designs and basic color options, catering to practical needs but lacking distinctive flair. However, as we embraced innovation and expanded our horizons, we significantly evolved our product offerings. We started our PVC laminate plant in 2017, and by 2019, we increased our production capacity to 500,000 sheets per annum, setting the stage for growth. In 2020, we launched the PVC HPL 3mm sheet, marking a key milestone in our commitment to excellence. Today, we offer a diverse range of designs, including sophisticated solid colors, intricate wood grains, and striking patterns inspired by natural stones like marble and granite. Our advanced manufacturing technologies ensure superior durability and aesthetic appeal, while our focus on exceptional customer experience provides personalized support and expert design advice. As we continue to innovate, our mission remains clear: to deliver products that transform spaces and exceed expectations.
